Our Story

Founded in 2020 in the heart of Coimbatore, PosterKadai began as a small passion project by a group of design enthusiasts who believed that everyone deserves access to beautiful, meaningful art.

What started as a weekend hobby has grown into a thriving creative community, combining traditional artistry with modern design techniques. We believe that posters are more than just wall decorations – they're storytellers, mood-setters, and conversation starters.

Our journey has been fueled by the joy of seeing our customers' faces light up when they receive their custom-designed posters. Every piece we create carries a piece of our passion and dedication to quality.
